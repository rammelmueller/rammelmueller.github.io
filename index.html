Hey there!

I'm Lukas Rammelmüller and currently pursuing a PhD in the group of Prof. Jens Braun @ TU Darmstadt. I'm a computational physicist and always happy when I can solve physics problems on a computer. My research focuses on ultracold atom systems and the development of efficient algorithms to study strongly correlated Fermi gases.

Previously I studied at TU Wien and I also had the pleasure of working at UNC Chapel Hill during several research stays.

If you are interested in my research or just want to say hello feel free to contact me!
